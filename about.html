History

"Embarking on its journey as a heartfelt birthday gift under the moniker 'VikhaHub,' this website emerged with laughter and a humorous video dedicated to a friend. Witnessing its evolution, we proudly introduce 'CarHub' a dynamic space born from the fusion of passion for cars and a classroom project. Here at 'CarHub,' we curate and showcase captivating car edits, transforming the initial joyous moments into a platform that celebrates creativity. What began as a personal gesture has evolved into a multifaceted project, embracing the fluidity of digital expression. Join us on this ever-evolving journey where laughter meets automotive artistry, and 'CarHub' continues to weave its unique narrative in the digital realm."
